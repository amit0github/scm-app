How It Works

Our Supply Chain Management platform streamlines the process of procurement and fulfillment by connecting Councils with vetted Contractors and Companies.

1. Project Creation

Councils identify needs for public works or services and create a new Project on the platform. They define specific Requirements (e.g., Materials, Labor, Logistics) needed for the project's completion.

2. Bidding Process

Contractors browse available Project Requirements suitable for their expertise. They submit competitive Bids detailing their cost and timeline. Companies can also manage large-scale projects and assign resources.

3. Selection & Execution

Councils review all bids and award contracts to the best candidates. Once selected, Contractors begin work, and progress is tracked through the platform until the Project is marked as 'Completed'.
